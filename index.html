Welcome to Halcyon Seas

Use this map to help choose the perfect get away

7 days in the Bahamas

Take a week to cruise around the Bahamas. You'll visit sandy white beaches with crystal clear water. Spend an afternoon snorkeling amongst coral reefs and dining on beach cabanas.

10 days in New England

The Autumn color of coastal Maine is one of the most beautiful natural wonders of the world. Take ten days to weave among the archipeligo off the coast of Acadia National park and experience the dramatic landscapes from the comforts of the boat. You'll get up close and personal with dozens of small islands.

14 days around the Mid Atlantic

The mid Atlantic has a lot to offer the casual yachter. From New York City's amazing harbor, head south off the coast of New Jersey to visit a number of cute towns, harbors, and beautiful landscapes.
